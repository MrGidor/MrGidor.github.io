Pulsar Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2024.09.16

At Pulsar, your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you interact with the Pulsar Discord bot ("Pulsar", "we", "our", or "us"). By using Pulsar, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with these practices, please refrain from using the bot.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our service:

a. User Data

When you interact with Pulsar, we may collect:

  • Discord User ID: A unique identifier assigned to your Discord account.
  • Server ID: The unique identifier of the Discord server where you interact with Pulsar.
  • Message Content: Specific commands or messages sent to Pulsar, as necessary to provide the requested functionality.
  • User Preferences: Any preferences you set when using Pulsar’s features (such as customized settings).

b. Usage Data

We may automatically collect data about your use of Pulsar, including:

  • Interaction logs (e.g., commands issued, usage patterns).
  • Time and frequency of interactions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve Pulsar’s functionality.
  • To respond to your queries or fulfill your requests for specific services.
  • To monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with Pulsar.
  • To ensure compliance with Discord’s Terms of Service and community guidelines.
  • To enhance your user experience by remembering your preferences and settings.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes. However, we may share information with:

  • Discord: As required by their platform policies, for moderation, or to investigate abuse.
  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the bot, subject to agreements ensuring the security of your data.
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law, court order, or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

5. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your information. We employ reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures designed to safeguard your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Choices

  • Opt-Out: You can choose to stop interacting with Pulsar at any time by ceasing to use the bot.
  • Data Deletion: You may request deletion of your data by contacting our support. Note that some data may be retained if it is required for legal purposes or operational integrity.
  • Access and Update: You may request access to or updates of your personal information by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

Pulsar is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child, we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. Continued use of Pulsar after changes have been made indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
